Alice Poker

Alice Poker is a 25-year-old town counsellor with an unfortunate habit of bumping off the people around her. She is Dutiful and Hard working, but can also be very Selfish and a bit Rude.

Her first victim was her friend, Claudia Dustin Jacobs, who she killed because he used a word she didn't particularly like. In 2020, she killed she half sister , Elsa.

She is German who defines herself as straight. She finished school and then left academia. She has a severe phobia of clowns, and is obsessed with films.

Physically, Alice is in pretty good shape. She is average-height with pale skin, brown hair and green eyes. She has Scratch on the cheek from tustle with Claudia Dustin Jacobs just before killing him..

She grew up in an upper class neighbourhood. Her mother left when she was young, leaving her with her father, who was a drunk.

She is currently in a relationship with Luther Frederick Ball. Luther is the same age as her and works as an IT technician.

Alice has one child with ex-boyfriend Denis: Christine aged 3.

Alice's best friend is a town counsellor called Isabelle Alexander. They are inseparable. She also hangs around with a town counsellor called Annette Barber. They enjoy golf together.
